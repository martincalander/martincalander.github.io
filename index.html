REIGNITE THE ART OF BOXING!


Big Boy Boxing is the action, slapstick, boss-rush game where every fight comes with a punchline. Challenge all the former boxing champions and reignite the glory days of boxing! Taking inspiration from old & new games like Shovel Knight, Cuphead & Punch-Out!!, you punch & dodge your way through over a dozen characters with unique gameplay concepts and a story/personality.
